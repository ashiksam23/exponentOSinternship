# Day 3 — Creative AI

**Date:** Thursday, May 14, 2026  
**Goal:** By the end of today, interns will have created a short AI-assisted written piece and a small themed AI image gallery.

## Kickoff (0:00 - 0:10)

**Warm-up:** "If AI could help you make any creative thing, what would you make?"

**Recap:** Yesterday we learned how to shape AI responses with better prompts. Today we apply that skill to creative work.

**Today's goal:** "You're the director. AI helps draft words and visuals, but you make the creative decisions."

## Learn Block (0:10 - 0:50)

### Concepts
- Co-creation: AI drafts, humans direct and edit
- Writing prompts: audience, voice, genre, structure
- Image prompts: subject, style, setting, lighting, composition
- Attribution: be honest about what AI helped create
- Taste matters: choosing, editing, and explaining your work

### Live Demo
Build a mini campaign for a fictional student club:

1. Ask AI for a club description
2. Revise the tone for teenagers
3. Generate 3 image prompts for posters
4. Improve one prompt with style and composition details

### Pause Points
- "What part still needs human judgment?"
- "Which image details are concrete enough?"
- "How would you credit AI help?"

## Build Block (1:00 - 1:50)

### Project: Creative AI Mini-Showcase

Create:

- One short story, blog post, diary entry, or explainer (300-600 words)
- Three themed AI image prompts
- At least one generated image
- A 3-5 sentence note explaining your creative choices

Suggested themes:

- A future school
- A helpful robot sidekick
- A public service campaign
- A fictional city in 2040
- A hobby you care about

**Stretch:** Create a matching title, subtitle, and social caption.

## Play Block (2:00 - 2:40)

### Activity: Story Remix Relay

In small groups:

1. Person A creates a story premise.
2. Person B prompts AI to twist the genre.
3. Person C creates an image prompt for the new version.
4. Group votes on the strongest remix.

## Share & Wrap (2:40 - 3:00)

**Share prompt:** "Show one sentence or image prompt you improved through iteration."

**Next-session preview:** "Tomorrow we talk about AI's limits, bias, and how to use these tools responsibly."

## Post-Session Notes

- Remind interns to keep creative samples for the portfolio support section.
- Award Storyteller and AI Artist candidates.
